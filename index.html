Food made with love

Growing up in Nigeria, I am pretty much surrounded by food all the time! Especially at home, alongside my mum and my grandma. Since a very young age, I have been helping out in the kitchen. I remember the very first time I peeled a potato, I peeled my finger as well! A painful yet memorable experience, as it made me want to learn cooking even more! Got the privilege to learn from the people I love.
